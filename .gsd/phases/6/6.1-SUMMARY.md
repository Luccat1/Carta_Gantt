# Plan 6.1 Summary: Multi-Month Calendar Selector

## Changes
- **Config.gs**: Added `DEFAULT_MONTH_END` constant.
- **Main.gs**: Updated `initStructure` to ensure the `CONFIG` sheet includes a `MesFin` row. Existing `CONFIG` sheets are automatically updated with `MesFin` defaulting to the same value as `Mes`.
- **Calendar.gs**: Extended `buildCalendarFromConfig` to loop through a range of months specified in `CONFIG`. It now handles year transitions (e.g., Nov to Feb) and populates the `LOOKUPS` sheet with sequential week rows (S1, S2, etc.).

## Verification Results
- Single-month behavior preserved when `Mes == MesFin`.
- Multi-month ranges (e.g., 2 months, 4 months) generated correctly in `LOOKUPS`.
- Year wrap logic tested: `year=2026, monthStart=11, monthEnd=2` correctly generates Nov-Dec 2026 and Jan-Feb 2027.
