## Lessons Learned
- **Architecture**: Separating logic into dedicated files (`Gantt.gs`, `Validation.gs`, `Rollover.gs`, etc.) kept `Main.gs` manageable despite 450+ lines of menu and init logic.
- **Header-map pattern**: Building `getHeaderMap_` early (Phase 4) prevented column-position bugs across all subsequent phases.
- **Wave execution**: Grouping plans into waves enabled parallel development within phases while respecting dependencies.
- **Gap closure**: A dedicated audit phase (11) caught a date format bug and naming confusion that would have caused production issues.
- **Documentation debt**: Maintaining TODO.md as a living document requires discipline — batch updates at milestone boundaries work best.
